Summary

The BI summer school 2024 will take place virtually (Zoom webinar) over two weekends in July (13th-14th and 20th-21st July). There will be lectures followed by Q&A on themes given below. The esteemed speakers are affiliated to or collaborators of the Bhaktivedanta Institute for Higher Studies (BIHS, Florida). The summer school is open to participants of all ages, but students and early-career professors of science and philosophy, and doctors of medicine, are especially encouraged to join. The Zoom link will be shared with everyone who submits the registration form (see below).

About

The Bhaktivedanta Institute (BI) was formed in 1976 to build intellectual bridges and create joint research paths between modern empirical science and the consciousness-based metaphysical framework of India’s Bhagavata Vedanta tradition. Over the last four decades, we have steadily built science- and philosophy-based research, education and outreach programs to investigate a wide gamut of topics encompassing mind, consciousness and Vedic cosmology. The Bhaktivedanta Institute for Higher Studies (BIHS) is a recently-formulated center in Gainesville, Florida.
